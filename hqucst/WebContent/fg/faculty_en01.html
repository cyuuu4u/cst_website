FACULTY

Shaobin Cai


Male, born in 1973, doctor of engineering, professor, doctoral tutor, the Ministry of education of the new century talents. He is currently the Dean of the School of Computer Science of Huaqiao University, Member of the Committee of Wireless Sensor Networks of the China Computer Society, Member of the Provincial Computer Society, and ACM Member of the United States. "Ad Hoc Network", "Computer Communications", "Journal of Software" and "Computer Research and Development" and other periodical reviewers, the National Natural Science Foundation, the Provincial Natural Science Foundation and small and medium enterprises innovation fund reviewers.


Respectively in 1996, 1998, 2005, at the Harbin Institute of Technology received bachelor, master and doctorate;


In 2006, he was supported by the first batch of young backbone teachers of Harbin Engineering University.


2008 to 2009, at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) engaged in postdoctoral research work;


In 2009, by the Heilongjiang province academic young backbone teachers support;


In 2013, the Ministry of Education of the new century talent program support.


Many times to the United States and Japan famous universities and research institutions for academic exchange visits and cooperative research, mainly engaged in network security, routing, MAC, reliable data transmission, node positioning and QoS technology research.


At present, as the project leader, presided over the National Natural Science Foundation 4, the Ministry of education doctoral fund 1, the national postdoctoral special funding 1, the provincial technical research project 1, the provincial natural science fund 1, the national postdoctoral fund 2, City Fund 1, the Ministry of Education Fundamental Research Fund 2, research funding over 700 million. Access to provincial and ministerial level scientific and technological progress 2 prize 1. The main research results are published in "Ad Hoc Network", "Computer Communications", "Computer Research and Development" and "Journal of Electronics" and other famous journals and conferences, a total of more than 80 articles. Among them, more than 20 SCI retrieval, EI and ISTP retrieval of more than 50 articles.
